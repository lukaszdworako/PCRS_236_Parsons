How to use:
  • Left-click to add a state
  • Clicking on a state and ending on another draws a line
  • Click on a state/line to select it
  • Press del or backspace to delete lines/states
  • Press 'a' on a state to make it accepting
  • Press a character on a line to make it accept that.
  • When you're done, click the button to get your text output.
